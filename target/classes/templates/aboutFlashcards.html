Study Mode

Study Mode of AnatomyApp allows you to go through flashcards sets with different anatomical structures. Each of them has an image with highlighted anatomical structure and a name in English, Latin and Polish.
Step 1: Choose a set of flashcards...
Step 2: ...view all flashcards in the set one by one...
Step 3: ...until the end. Congratulations!
Step 4: Now you can get back to the main page, choose another set to study or check your knowledge in Test Mode.
